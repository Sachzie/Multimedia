About Me

Hello, I'm Sachzie Sofia Ilagan

I am a student from the Technological University of the Philippines, currently enrolled in BSIT-NS-2A.

Through this portfolio, I present some of my early works in Blender — from simulating the solar system to crafting simple visual storytelling pieces. One of the highlights of my journey is experimenting with rotoscope animation— a frame-by-frame technique where I trace over video footage to create stylized, hand-drawn motion. This project helped me understand movement, timing, and visual rhythm while blending traditional animation principles with digital tools.
